Top 3 Sales Plays

Win the workload where DDN can prove measurable advantage.

Rank 1 · EXAScaler

Accelerate trading research, replay, and checkpointing.

Establish EXAScaler as the performance layer for market replay, backtesting, simulation, AI training, model loading, and checkpointing. This connects most directly to research velocity and trading edge.

Proof Points
  • Faster access to historical market datasets under concurrency.
  • Reduced checkpoint and restore time for AI/HPC workloads.
  • Low variability under load, not only peak benchmark throughput.
  • Strength with many files, many jobs, and research-heavy access patterns.
90-Day Outcome

Identify one high-value replay, simulation, or checkpointing workload; capture baseline metrics; secure technical sponsor; define POC criteria.

Rank 2 · Infinia

Build the governed AI data and regulatory archive layer.

Expand the current Infinia opportunity into a governed object, AI data, and compliance retention architecture for market history, model artifacts, research outputs, logs, simulation results, and retained records.

Proof Points
  • Immutable storage and policy-based retention.
  • Fast retrieval for retained market and trading data.
  • Shared AI datasets, feature-store-like workflows, and model artifacts.
  • Encryption, access control, policy isolation, and multi-team separation.
90-Day Outcome

Clarify the Infinia Q226 use case, validate buyer and owner, build fit/no-fit matrix, quantify data footprint and retention requirements.

Rank 3 · EXAScaler + Infinia + Managed Lustre

Shape the hybrid HPC architecture.

Help Citadel define where performance data should live across on-prem, cloud, and managed cloud-adjacent environments without sacrificing performance, cost control, or governance.

Proof Points
  • Hybrid workload map across file, object, archive, and cloud-adjacent data.
  • Clear view of cloud movement cost, data gravity, and latency constraints.
  • Managed Lustre only where a GCP-adjacent Lustre workflow is validated.
  • Target architecture for one hybrid AI/HPC workflow.
120-Day Outcome

Validate Managed Lustre relevance, identify cloud cost or data movement pain, and define the right DDN product combination.
